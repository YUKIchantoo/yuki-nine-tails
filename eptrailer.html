3. Camera & Animation Notes

View Notes
  • Slow pans and gentle dolly-ins for a dreamy feel.
  • Foxfire particles drifting upward with soft glow.
  • Snowfall with depth-of-field blur for parallax.
  • Light bloom on emotional beats and title reveal.
  • Constellation animation using line-draw then fill.
  • Tracklist flashes synced to beat pulses.
  • Final foxfire engulf using additive glow and fade.

4. Music & Sound Design Guide

View Audio Guide
  • Intro: Soft wind, single chime, low ambient pad.
  • 0:12: Heartbeat-like synth enters under close-up.
  • 0:28: Beat drop with subtle percussion and bass swell.
  • 0:36–0:52: Emotional rise, strings or pads building.
  • 0:56: Crescendo with foxfire engulf.
  • 1:00: Fade to a single chime and silence.

11. Editor Notes

General Instructions
  • Keep total runtime around 1:00.
  • Sync visual intensity with music dynamics.
  • Prioritize readability of on-screen text.
  • Use smooth crossfades and light-based transitions.
  • Maintain consistent foxfire color palette (blue/white with soft glow).
