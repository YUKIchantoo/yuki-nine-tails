The Lore Behind the EP

Beneath the moonlit sky, a Kitsune wanders — carrying the weight of unspoken wishes, forgotten memories, and the soft ache of human emotion.

This EP captures the moment when the ninth tail flickers awake, igniting a transformation that blends myth and reality. Each track is a fragment of that awakening — a spark of foxfire breaking through the darkness.

Dual Narration Concept

The EP is told through two intertwined voices:

  • The World Voice — a deep, resonant baritone narrating the myth, the setting, the fate.
  • The Voice of Wishes — your voice, soft and emotional, expressing the heart of the Kitsune.

Together, they form a story of longing, identity, and rebirth.
